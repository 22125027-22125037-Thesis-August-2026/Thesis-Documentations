# uMatter — The Mobile App 💚

### *Tâm lý · Chữa lành · Kết nối* — Mind · Healing · Connection

> **A safe space to care for your mental health — every day, your own way.**
>
> uMatter is a mental-wellness companion built for teenagers and young people. It turns looking after
> your mind into a gentle daily habit: notice how you feel, understand your patterns, talk to someone
> (a caring AI any time, or a verified therapist when you need more), and keep the moments that make
> you feel good close by. No judgment. No pressure. 💚

*This page is a product tour — it showcases what the app **does** for the person using it. For the
engineering behind it, see [03-Frontend/Mobile-App](../03-Frontend/Mobile-App.md).*

---

## 🌙 1. Check in with your feelings — and get gently reminded to

The heart of uMatter is the **daily mood check-in**. With one tap on the home screen
("**Cảm xúc lúc này** — How are you feeling right now?") you log how you feel across five moods —
from **TỆ HẠI** (terrible) to **TUYỆT VỜI** (wonderful) — and add a quick line about why.

These small check-ins are the thread that ties everything together: over days and weeks they become a
picture of your emotional life that powers your charts, your journal, your AI companion, and (if you
choose) the therapist who supports you.

**✨ Smart, caring reminders — "Chế độ tập trung" (Focus Mode).**
Turn on Focus Mode and uMatter sends a **gentle nudge roughly every 90 minutes** during your day:
- 💙 *"Đừng quên ghi lại cảm xúc của bạn hôm nay nhé!"* — a nudge to capture how you're feeling, which
  taps straight into your journal.
- 💚 *"Hãy uống một ngụm nước và hít thở sâu nhé!"* — a tiny self-care reminder to sip water and breathe.

It's considerate by design: a **Quiet Hours** window (default **10 PM–7 AM**) means it never disturbs
you while you sleep, and the reminders quietly refill themselves so you stay supported without ever
being nagged. *This is the feature that keeps mood-tracking effortless — the app comes to you.*

And when you log a low mood, uMatter responds with care — offering a calming prompt or a path to
support rather than leaving you alone with it.

## 📔 2. "Góc tâm tư" — your emotional journal

**Góc tâm tư** ("a corner for your thoughts") is your private feelings diary — *"Chuyện của mình"*,
your story.

- Write a title, pick the mood that fits, and pour out *"Bạn đang nghĩ gì?"* — whatever's on your mind.
  Up to **5 photos** per entry. *"Không có gì là sai cả!"* — nothing you feel is wrong. 💫
- A **365-day journey**: watch your entries build across the year, with a **mood calendar**
  ("Dòng cảm xúc") that colours each day **positive / neutral / negative** at a glance.
- **Streaks** keep you motivated (🔥 *X ngày* in a row), and you can **search** or filter by date to
  revisit any moment.

It's a judgment-free habit of self-reflection — the kind that quietly helps you understand yourself.

## 🎁 3. "Hộp kho báu" — your treasure box of good memories

> *"Cất giữ ảnh, lời nhắn và những điều khiến bạn thấy ấm lòng — mở ra mỗi khi cần được an ủi."*
> Keep the photos, the messages, and the little things that warm your heart — and open them whenever
> you need comforting.

The **Treasure Box** is uMatter's most heartfelt feature. On a hard day, low moods can make it hard to
remember anything good ever happened. So uMatter gives you a place to **save your positive core
memories** ahead of time — a photo, a kind word, a small win — and return to them exactly when you
need a lift. It's self-compassion you can hold in your hand. 💛

## 🧘 4. Take care of your whole self

uMatter knows mental health isn't just mood — it's sleep, movement, food, and breath. Each lives on
your home screen as a friendly "today" card, with beautiful **7-day trends** behind it.

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| 😴 **Giấc ngủ** (Sleep) | Log bedtime, wake time, and how you slept (5 levels, from *7–9 hrs* great to *<3 hrs*). See your weekly sleep quality at a glance. |
| 👟 **Bước chân** (Steps) | **Automatically counts your steps** in the background, with a daily goal, distance & calories, and a *"Đã đạt mục tiêu! 🎉"* celebration when you hit it. |
| 🍎 **Dinh dưỡng** (Nutrition) | Log meals (breakfast/lunch/dinner/snack), your **water intake** in litres, and how full you felt — even **mindful eating** ("Ăn chánh niệm"). |
| 🌬️ **Hít thở** (Breathing) | *"Một khoảnh khắc để tâm trí bình yên."* Guided breathing — **inhale, hold, exhale** — with calming music and reflective prompts (picture a happy memory, someone you love, a safe place). |
| 🎧 **Thiền & thư giãn** (Meditation) | A curated carousel of short sessions: **4-7-8 breathing**, **body scan**, **morning**, **sleep**, and **calm-anxiety** — bite-sized resets made for you. |

Every log you make also feeds your **daily achievements** (below) — so taking care of yourself feels
rewarding, not like a chore.

## 🤖 5. "Bạn Tâm Giao" — an AI friend who's always listening

> *"Trợ lý AI thấu cảm, sẵn sàng trò chuyện 24/7 — không phán xét, không áp lực."*
> An empathetic AI, ready to talk 24/7 — no judgment, no pressure.

**Bạn Tâm Giao** (your "soul-mate companion") is an AI you can talk to any time — at 3 AM, before an
exam, after a fight. What makes it special: it's **grounded in *your* context**. Because it can draw
on how you've actually been sleeping and feeling, its replies are personal, not generic.

- Always *"đang trực tuyến"* (online), with a warm, gentle voice: *"Mình luôn ở đây lắng nghe bạn 💚"*.
- Quick-reply chips to get started — *"Mình ổn 😊" · "Buồn một chút" · "Lo lắng quá"*.
- **Safety first:** if the conversation suggests you're in crisis, uMatter responds with care and
  surfaces a **real support hotline (1800 599 920)** immediately.

## 🩺 6. Find the right therapist — and meet them from your phone

When you want more than self-help, uMatter connects you with a **verified psychologist** matched to
*you*.

1. **Smart matching ("ghép nối").** An 8-step, caring intake asks what matters: have you tried therapy
   before, what you're going through (anxiety, low mood, study stress, insomnia, relationships, trauma,
   eating), how you're feeling right now, whether you'd like an **LGBTQ+-allied** therapist, and the
   communication style you prefer (a **listener**, a **guide**, or both). The system then pairs you
   with a compatible, verified professional.
2. **Book a session** in a tap — pick a free slot on the therapist's calendar.
3. **Meet by video or chat** — *"Sẵn sàng kết nối với chuyên gia."* Join a secure video room, or
   message in text, right from the app.
4. **After the session**, read your therapist's notes (diagnosis & recommendations), see your next
   steps, and **leave a rating & review** to help others.

You're always in control of your appointments — confirm, view details, or cancel with a reason — and
you're kept in the loop by both a **push notification and an email** the moment your therapist confirms
(or if a session is cancelled), so a change to your plans never slips past you.

## 🏆 8. Small wins, celebrated

Looking after yourself should feel good — so uMatter turns it into gentle, rewarding progress:

- **Cúp tracking hôm nay** (Today's Trophy): complete your daily categories — **nutrition, sleep,
  diary, steps** — to earn a **bronze, silver, or gold** cup. *"Tuyệt vời! Hoàn thành hôm nay 🎉"*
- **Streaks** for your journal and habits keep momentum going.
- Your **Profile** shows off your achievements: journal streak, AI chat sessions, and "member since".
- Little **celebration moments** pop up when you hit a milestone.

## 🔒 10. Private by design, and made for you

- **You decide who sees your data.** Your journal, mood, and sleep are **yours**. A therapist can only
  view them **if you explicitly grant permission** — and you can revoke it at any time
  (*"Người được xem nhật ký của bạn"*).
- **Bilingual** — fully in **Tiếng Việt** and **English**.
- **Your look** — light, dark, or system theme.
- **A friendly first-run tour** walks you through every feature in under a minute (and you can replay
  it whenever you like).
- **A home-screen widget** keeps your day a glance away.

> uMatter is a *support* companion, not an emergency service — and it says so plainly. In a real
> emergency it points you straight to professional help.

## 🧠 11. Why these features work — and who helped us build them

*uMatter isn't a list of features we guessed at. Each pillar of the app was reviewed with practising
mental-health professionals, and each one is grounded in **why** it actually helps a young person's
wellbeing. Sections 1–10 are the product tour; this part is the "why" behind them.*

**🏃 Movement & breathing — guided by Cô Phạm Thị Thanh Quí** *(counsellor, Psychological Counselling
Office, University of Science, VNU-HCM).*
Her core point: physical and mental health are inseparable. So the **automatic step counter** and the
**breathing exercises** (§4) do double duty — they give a therapist real signal to track your activity
and assign suitable exercises, *and* they let you lift your own mental wellbeing by caring for your
body. Breath regulation in particular is one of the most evidence-backed ways to calm anxiety in the
moment.

**🎁 The Treasure Box — suggested by Anh Vương Nguyễn Toàn Thiện** *(CEO, LUMOS).*
When we feel low, the mind tends to surface only what's negative — good memories become hard to reach.
His insight, well-supported by psychological research on positive memory and mood, is that you should
**store your core positive memories somewhere safe ahead of time** and reopen them exactly when you
need a lift. That is the entire reason the **Treasure Box** (§3) exists: a reliable anchor back to the
good.

**🆘 Crisis safety — shaped by BS. Phạm Trần Thành Nghiệp** *(physician, Hoàn Mỹ Sài Gòn Hospital),
with input from Anh Thiện on the SOS area.*
From a clinical-safety standpoint, an app for vulnerable teens must never leave someone alone in a
crisis. That is why **Bạn Tâm Giao detects crisis signals and surfaces a real hotline (1800 599 920)
immediately** (§5), and why the **emergency-support / SOS area is always one tap away** (§9) — routing
you to professional help the moment it matters.
